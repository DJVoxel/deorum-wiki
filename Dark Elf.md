## Appearance
Dark Elf – Has pointed ears, light purple skin and is slightly taller than humans (190cm). Eyes can also be red or yellow in addition to normal colours. 
## Abilities
Dark Elf – Strong – Nature magic starts at 50 and Dark magic starts at 25. Learns Dark magic 25% faster and Nature magic 10% faster, can understand manifestations and monsters (starts with a 'dark’ or monster familiar) – Weak – fire magic does 50% more damage, light magic does 25% more damage and learns both 50% slower.
