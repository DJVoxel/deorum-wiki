## Appearance
Frost Elf – Has pointed ears, light blue skin and is slightly taller than humans (190cm). Eyes can also be dark blue or yellow in addition to normal colours. 
## Abilities
Frost Elf – Strong – Nature magic starts at 50 and Ice magic starts at 50. Learns Ice magic 50% faster and Nature magic 25% faster, can understand all animals (starts with a familiar) – Weak – fire magic does 75% more damage, and learns it 100% slower.
