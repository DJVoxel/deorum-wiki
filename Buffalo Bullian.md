## Appearance
Buffalo Bullian - strong - Human like but has 2 great big horns on its head, can be curled. Have hooves. Also have bigger physiques than normal Bullian and a shaggy coat of hair on their bodies.
## Personality
Buffalo Bullian are very honest, sincere, and kind, they are ready to help others but forget their own interests. In life, they have a high degree of independence, and absolutely do not like to rely on others. When they’re not working, Buffalo Bullian like chilling in hot springs and mud baths.
## Abilities
(Buffalo) Bullian – Strong – When charging, agility is multiplied by 3, hitting does a lot of damage to their opponent and magic will affect them less while charging. 30 agility and 60 strength at birth. Learns both 50% faster – weak – Missing their target and hitting something from the environment could knock them out but are more resistant to it than normal Bullian. Takes 75% more damage from electricity magic.
