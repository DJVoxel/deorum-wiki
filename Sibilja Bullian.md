
## Appearance

- 2 great big silver horns on its head
  - can be curled
- silver hooves

## Personality
Sibilja Bullian are a mystery. Some are bold, adventurous and temperamental, while others may be shy and timid, yet calm.

## Abilities
- Has the ability to Charge:
  - [[Stats#Physcial Stats|Agility]] is doubled
  - Deals large damage upon hitting an opponent
  - Incoming magic has no effect
  -  Colliding with the environment can KO them
- Starts with 30 [[Stats#Physcial Stats|Agility]].
- Starts with 50 [[Sound Magic]] 
  - [[Sound Magic]] is gained 50% faster
- Can learn [[God Arts]]
- Learns all other [[Magic]] 10% faster
- Immune to [[Status#Insanity|Insanity]]
- 50% Weakness to [[Electric Magic]]
- Learns all [[Stats#Physcial Stats|Physical Stats]] aside from Agility 25% slower
