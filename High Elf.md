## Appearance
High Elf – Has pointed ears, pale caucasian skin and is slightly taller than humans (190cm). Eyes are always golden yellow and have black hair, with silvery hues.
## Abilities
High Elf – Strong – Light magic starts at 75 and God Arts at 10. Learns Light magic and God Arts 25% faster. Can understand all animals (starts with a royal/angelic familiar) – Weak – dark magic does 50% more damage, and they cannot learn it.
