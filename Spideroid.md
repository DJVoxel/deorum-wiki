## Appearance
Spideroid – has 8 spider legs coming out of their waist (colour of legs are their hair colour).
## Abilities
Spideroid – Strong – Has 8 additional spider limbs. trap magic and web magic start at 25 and are learnt 50% faster. Once webs are out they can move faster [increases with web magic], can feel where on the web someone touches the web. Agility and strength are learnt 10% faster – Weak - Can’t learn nature magic and is weak to nature by 33%. Learns flexibility and dexterity 30% slower. +
