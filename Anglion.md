## Appearance
- Has wings
- Halo
## Personality
Anglions are normally a serious race that won’t joke around unless they know you on a personal level. The main difference between males and females is that females are very benevolent and soft spoken while the males are much more blunt and ‘cold’.

## Abilities
- [[Light Magic]] starts at 50.
	- Can heal with light and use light movement (teleportation) at start
	- Can form weapons from light magic for a fraction of the normal cost
- Learns [[Light Magic]] 25% faster.
- All weapons start at 20.
- All Weapon Skills match the current highest Weapon Skill.
- Has Wings
- [[Stats|Agility]] or [[Stats|Strength]] starts at 300 and the chosen one is learnt 25% faster
- Learns all other magic 90% slower
- Cannot learn [[Dark Magic]]
- 10% Weakness to [[Dark Magic]]
