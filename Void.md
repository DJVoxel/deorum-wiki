## Abilities
- Can take out someone's void (Soul-weapon) through (mostly) physical contact
- Two stats of choice start at 50, the rest start at 22 (including sensory stats, [[Stats|Flexibility]] and [[Stats|Dexterity]])
- Learn all [[Stats#Physical Stats|Physical Stats]] faster based on the gap between a physical stat and your highest physical stat (Larger-Smaller=%)
- Learn how to use weapons 100% faster.
	- Weapon type of choice gains an additional 20% learn speed.
- Can't use any magic unless the void has it
- Voids will vanish after a time has passed. (usually not mid-combat)
