## Appearance
Blood Elf – Has pointed ears, light red or pink skin and is slightly taller than humans (190cm). Eyes can also be bright red or bright green in addition to normal colours.
## Abilities
Blood Elf – Strong – Nature magic starts at 50 and Blood magic starts at 50. Learns Blood magic 50% faster and Nature magic 25% faster, can understand undead creatures/monsters (starts with a 'blood' or undead familiar) – Weak – Fire and Ice magic do 50% more damage, and learns both 50% slower.
