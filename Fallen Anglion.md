## Abilities
- [[Dark Magic]] starts at 70.
  - Can heal with [[Dark Magic]] and [[Dark Magic#Movement (Shadowing)|Shadow]]
- [[Dark Magic]] is learned 20% faster.
- All weapon skills start at 20
- Has Wings
- [[stats#Physcial Stats|Reflexes]] or [[stats#Physcial Stats|Agility]] starts at 250
- Learns all other [[Magic]] 70% slower
- Cannot learn [[Light Magic]] nor [[Nature Magic]]
- 10% Weakness to [[Light Magic]]
