## Appearence
Hair, eyes, nails and blood are all one chosen colour. Eyes and nails glow. Aether shares the same base colour.
## Abilities
- Starts with [[Aether Magic]] at 200 and learns it 15% faster.
- Learns Exterior and Weapon Skills 20% Faster.
- Raising any non-Aether [[Magic]] to 50 increases resistance to all Magical Damage by 3%, but next magic is learnt 10% slower.
- Physical and Magical [[Stats|Stamina]] are shared.
