## Appearance
Rhino Bullian - Human like but has 1 big rhino horn on its head, can have another smaller horn below or above. Have bulkier bodies with tough/armored skin. 
## Personality
Rhino Bullian are said to have a variable personality. They can be calm, stubborn, curious, and explosive at random moments. At the same time, they don't care much about the assessment of others and love to remain cautious.
## Abilities
(Rhino) Bullian – Strong – When charging, agility and strength are multiplied by 3, hitting someone with this does a lot of damage to their opponent. 50 strength at birth. Learns strength 50% faster. Has tough skin that resists physical attacks and magic by 40%. – weak – Missing their target and hitting something from the environment has a higher chance to knock them out. Takes 50% more damage from electricity and fire magic (these pierce its 40% magic resistance) and projectile/ranged attacks such as guns do 25% more damage.
